Best Career options for Medical student

MBBS
Bachelor of Medicine and Bachelor of Surgery

it is a professional undergraduate degree in the medical field. After completing MBBS, one can pursue a career as a medical doctor, surgeon, or physician. The degree generally takes five and a half years to complete, including a one-year internship.
BDS
Bachelor of Dental Surgery

is a popular undergraduate degree program in the field of dentistry. BDS graduates are trained to provide dental care and treatment to patients.BDS graduates may also choose to specialize in specific areas of dentistry such as orthodontics, periodontics, endodontics, prosthodontics.
BAMS
Bachelor of Ayurvedic Medicine and Surgery

It is an undergraduate degree program in the field of Ayurvedic medicine, which is a traditional system of medicine that originated in India.The BAMS course is designed to provide students with a comprehensive understanding of Ayurvedic principles, practices, and treatments.
BHMS
Bachelor of Homeopathic Medicine and Surgery

The course is designed to provide students with in-depth knowledge of the principles and practices of homeopathy.They also gain a thorough understanding of the human body and its functioning, diagnosis and treatment of various diseases using homeopathic remedies.
B.VSc
Bachelor of veterinary science

It is an undergraduate degree program that prepares students for a career in veterinary medicine. It typically takes around five years to complete and covers topics such as animal anatomy and physiology, pharmacology, surgery, animal behavior, and animal welfare.
BUMS
Bachelor of Unani Medicine and Surgery

It is a professional degree program in the field of Unani medicine. It is a 5.5-year undergraduate course that includes 1 year of compulsory internship. The course covers various subjects such as anatomy, physiology, pathology, pharmacology, surgery, and Unani medicine.
B Pharm
Bachelor of Pharmacy

It is an undergraduate academic degree program in the field of pharmacy. The course generally takes four years to complete and is designed to provide students with a strong foundation in pharmaceutical sciences, drug development, pharmacology, and clinical pharmacy.
BPT
Bachelor of Physiotherapy

It is an undergraduate program that prepares students to become physiotherapists. It takes four years to complete and covers subjects such as anatomy, physiology, pathology, kinesiology, biomechanics, neurology, musculoskeletal, cardiorespiratory and pediatric physiotherapy.
FAQ

Q1: Which top Medical colleges in India have the lowest fee structure?

Approximately, 41 percent of the top Medical colleges in India have a total tuition fee of less than INR 1 lakh. The top Medical colleges along with their tuition fee are AIIMS Delhi, Postgraduate Institute of Medical Education and Research, Chandigarh, Jawaharlal Institute of Postgraduate Medical Education and Research, Pondicherry, JSS Medical College, Mysore, and Christian Medical College, Vellore.

Q2: Which are the top specialisations offered by the top Medical colleges in India?

The Medical colleges in India offer various specialisations. The top specialisations offered in top Medical colleges are Cardiology, Cardiovascular Technology, Echo Cardiology and Cardiac Technology, Emergency Medicine, Operation Theatre Technology, Perfusion Technology, and many more.

Q3: Which are the most popular entrance exams accepted by the top Medical Colleges in India?

The top Medical colleges in India accept scores obtained by students in numerous national-level examinations. About 31 best Medical colleges accept NEET scores, 3 best Medical colleges accept NEET PG scores, and some of the top Medical colleges also accept AP NEET scores, INI CET scores, NEET SS scores, and many more.

Q4: Which top Medical colleges offer the highest package in India?

The top Medical colleges that offer the highest package in India along with their annual average package are Sree Chitra Tirunal Institute for Medical Sciences and Technology with an INR 23 LPA package, Sanjay Gandhi Postgraduate Institute of Medical Sciences with an INR 22 LPA package, Jawaharlal Institute of Postgraduate Medical Education and Research with INR 12 LPA package, AIIMS Jodhpur with INR 11.03 package, and IMS BHU with INR 11 LPA package.
