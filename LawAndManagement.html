Law And Management

The field of Law and Management is an interdisciplinary course that combines aspects of law and business management. It is designed to provide students with the knowledge and skills needed to manage legal issues in a business environment. Law and Management courses cover a wide range of topics, including business law, contracts, torts, intellectual property law, employment law, regulatory compliance, corporate governance, financial management, marketing, operations management, and strategic planning.
