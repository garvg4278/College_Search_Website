Jain University

Jain (Deemed to be) University, previously known as Sri Bhagawan Mahaveer Jain College (SBMJC) was established in 1990. Jain University's (JU) Ranking as per NIRF 2022 is 79th in the University category and 115th in the Engineering category. It is also rated by NAAC as an A++ institute with a CGPA of 3.71. The university is popular for its MBA and MCA courses. The total fee for the MBA program is INR 9.9 Lacs and INR 4.5 Lacs for the MCA program. The University also provides online degrees in several disciplines through its distance learning platform. The highest package offered in the year 2023 was INR 30 LPA and the average package stood at INR 6.92 LPA. Notable Alumni of Jain University are KL Rahul (Cricketer), Sharath Gayakwad (Swimmer), and other reputed personalities. Jain University Admissions to all the UG and PG courses are based on Jain Entrance Test (JET) conducted by the university.
