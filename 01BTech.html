Indian Institute of Technology (IIT), Bombay

Detail Value
Fee Structure The fee structure for B.Tech programs at IIT Bombay varies for different categories. For General/OBC category, the fee is around Rs. 2-3 lakhs per year, while for SC/ST category, it is significantly lower.
Admission Criteria Admissions to IIT Bombay's B.Tech programs are based on the JEE Advanced entrance exam. Candidates must qualify JEE Advanced and also meet the eligibility criteria set by the institute.
Eligibility Candidates must have passed the 10+2 examination or its equivalent with Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics as core subjects. They should also qualify JEE Main and JEE Advanced exams.
NIRF Ranking Rank 1
Location Powai, Mumbai, Maharashtra
Official Website https://www.iitb.ac.in/

Indian Institute of Technology (IIT), Kharagpur

Detail Value

Birla Institute of Technology and Science (BITS), Pilani

Detail Value
Fee Structure The fee structure for B.Tech programs at BITS Pilani varies for different campuses. For the academic year 2022-2023, the approximate fee for the Pilani campus is around Rs. 4.23 lakhs per year.
Admission Criteria Admissions to the B.Tech programs at BITS Pilani are based on the BITS Admission Test (BITSAT). Candidates need to qualify BITSAT and participate in the counseling process for seat allotment.
Eligibility Candidates should have passed the 10+2 examination or its equivalent with Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics as core subjects. They should also have a minimum aggregate score of 75% in Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics, with at least 60% marks in each subject. Candidates also have to appear in the BITSAT exam.
NIRF Ranking Rank 11
Location Pilani, Rajasthan, with campuses in Goa, Hyderabad, and Dubai.
Official Website https://www.bits-pilani.ac.in/
