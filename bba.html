BBA (Bachelor of Arts)

B.A. (Bachelor of Arts) degree is an undergraduate academic degree that usually takes three to four years to complete. The course provides a broad education in the humanities, social sciences, languages, and fine arts.Graduates of a B.A. program can go on to pursue further education, such as a master's or doctoral degree, or enter the workforce in a variety of fields, including education, government, journalism, business, and more.
